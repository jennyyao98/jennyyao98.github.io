Hi, I'm Jenny Yao.

Check out my Resume

A student, designer, and developer, I've always had a fascination towards design and the visual interface of the web. "Why do a website look a certain way" is always a question on my mind when I'm browsing.

Currently, I am a 4th year student at the University of Virginia majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Studio Arts.

Recently, this past summer, I interned at ASM Research as a UX/UI Design Intern to help them design an up and rising project called MEDCHART-U. Currently, at university, I am on a UI design team for the development of a ROCA-1, a Course Observation Tool for the Education School at the University fo Virginia.
