Hi, I'm Jenny Yao.

Check out my Resume

As a designer, I'm always had a fascination towards design and the visual interfaces of the web. "Why do a website look a certain way" is always a question on my mind when I'm browsing.

My design philosophy is that designing is like drafting an essay, every iteration gets better. However, there is never a final design. It is living, breathing, and constantly changing to meet the user's needs. Being a part of that design process means constantly improving, not perfecting, the user's experience.
