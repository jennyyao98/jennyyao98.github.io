Overview

LMS is an E-University under the department called MEDCHART (Medical Electronic Data Care History and Readiness Tracking). In order to login into the MEDCHART webpage the user must have two permissions, the HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996) and the CAC(Common Access Card) certifications. The LMS provides CAC authorized users the ability to take required (HIPAA Exam) and non-required assessment and courses ("rosters") by the government.
